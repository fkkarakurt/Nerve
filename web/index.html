02 — on-device learning · the part the cloud can't do

It learns from you, in milliseconds

Give it a few of your own examples per category, press Teach, then type anything. It tells you where it belongs and how sure it is — and admits when it doesn't know. Edit the categories to make it truly yours.

Nerve — a single-header, zero-dependency neural-network engine in pure C, compiled to a 65 KB WebAssembly module.
The model (~15 MB) loads once; after that everything runs offline, on your hardware, with your data. Open your network tab — it stays silent.
