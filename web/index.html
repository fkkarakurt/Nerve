2. It learns from you — the part the cloud can't do

Give it a few of your own examples per category, press Teach, and it learns them in milliseconds — privately, on your machine. Then type anything and it sorts it. This is an AI that remembers what you teach it.

Powered by Nerve — a single-header, zero-dependency neural-network engine in pure C, compiled to a 65 KB WebAssembly module.
The model (~15 MB) loads once; everything after that runs offline, on your hardware, with your data.
